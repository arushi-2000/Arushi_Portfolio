Intro

My concept revolves around a multifaceted and independent girl who embraces life with joy while fulfilling various roles in society. She seamlessly balances being a daughter, sister, friend, and girlfriend, presenting a harmonious exterior. Despite her outward happiness, there's a hidden complexity, as she keeps her true desires and feelings private. The metaphor of living freely like a bird suggests a longing for unrestricted freedom. This narrative highlights the societal expectations placed on individuals, especially women, and the tension between conforming to these expectations and seeking personal liberation. It explores the idea that even those who seem content may harbor unexpressed desires and aspirations.

Concept Board

My story line goes a group of girls engaging in various enjoyable activities, capturing the essence of carefree moments and the camaraderie among friends. As they chat about their lives, hang out with pets, and indulge in food and drinks, the scenes radiate a sense of joy and connection. The "Netflix and chill" moments reflect a relaxed and entertaining time spent together. The storyline takes a liberating turn as the girls venture out to the beach, embodying a spirit of freedom and seizing the moments of their girlhood. This narrative beautifully captures the simple pleasures and the bonds shared among friends during this carefree period of life.
